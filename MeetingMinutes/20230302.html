Discussed Points
Background
The old version of IR controllers (e.g. SE, SG, SG.N, CMC) are using MODBUS protocol as the communication protocol.

The new version of IR controllers (e.g. SCi/SCn) are using Mitsubishi protocol, (e.g., Luminance) are using MODBUS RTU protocol.

There are many kinds of communion protocol (e.g. Canbus, Modbus, OPC DA, OPC UA, Profibus, RS232, RS485, etc.). Modbus is one of the most commonly used protocol among them.

Since then, it is easily for the industrial integration service provider (e.g. Emerson, GE, Honeywell, Rockwell, Schneider Electric, Siemens, etc.) or even customers themselves to build their solution(s) or use their SCADA software to capture machine data from our products installed at customers' site.

There also gives our competitors the possibilities to generate the service leads/opportunities by leveraging the machine data of our products and then predict for the upcoming maintenance which may have negative impact on our aftermarket service business.

One of our competitors (e.g. Atlas Copco) is using their private Canbus protocol.

From business perspective, if we could have our own private protocol, then we would have more control of data.
